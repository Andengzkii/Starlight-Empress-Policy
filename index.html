Starlight Empress Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 13, 2024

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle any information you might provide while using our Starlight Empress app. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.

Information Collection

The Starlight Empress app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users.

1. Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal information such as device type, operating system, and user interaction with the app. This information helps us improve app performance and user experience.

2. Personal Information

No personal information is required or collected by the Starlight Empress app. We do not gather names, contact details, or any other personal data.

Use of Collected Information

As no personal information is collected, we do not use or share personal data. Non-personal information is used exclusively to enhance app functionality and the overall user experience.

Third-Party Services

Starlight Empress does not incorporate third-party services that gather personal data. We do not employ analytics, advertising, or tracking services that monitor user behavior.

Security

We prioritize the security of your information. Although our app does not collect personal data, we have safeguards in place to protect any non-personal information from unauthorized access or exposure.

Children's Privacy

Starlight Empress is accessible to users of all ages. We do not knowingly gather personal information from children under 13 years old. If we find that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will promptly delete it.

Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review the policy regularly to stay informed of any updates.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us.
